### Customization

The `@shopify/connect-wallet` has an extremely high level of customization allowing merchants and developers alike to fine-tune the appearance of their wallet connection integration.

Staying on brand is one of the most important boxes to tick for merchants, and that's exactly what we pushed for with our package.
